\section{Internal freeze approach} \label{sec:internal-freeze}
For both teams I was part of, it was helpful to have an internal "freeze day" in the form of a working session where
everything that could and should be done is checked.
Afterward, only new results should be uploaded, no design changes should be made, etc.
Alumni and professors can be invited to give input, proofread texts or help otherwise.
